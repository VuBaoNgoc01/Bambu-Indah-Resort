Immerse yourself in a riverside jungle retreat

No visit to Ubud is complete without spending a day at Bambu Indah, the vision of jewellery designer John Hardy who arrived in Bali in the 70’s and his wife Cynthia whom he met here ten years later. Peaceful, laidback and in harmony with nature, this ecologically mindful jungle retreat encapsulates old school Bali in a new way. From the food grown in the gardens, to the water from the ground and the energy from the sun, Bambu Indah lives and breathes in symphony with the surrounding gardens and jungle and invites you to join us for an otherworldly experience.

The Riverside Warung

Overlooking the spectacular Ayung River and spring-fed natural bathing pools, the riverside Warung is Bambu Indah’s signature restaurant. Delicious food sustainably and locally sourced is cooked in a traditional Balinese-style open air kitchen but it’s the dining room with roofs of treated bamboo bent into overlapping arches supporting a curved roof of hand-folded cand hammered opper shingles which catch the eye. “I wanted it to appear as though it was constructed by a highly intelligent bird,” explains Elora Hardy, John’s daughter and founder of Ibuku an innovative design and architectural company leading the way in working with bamboo and other sustainable materials. Open 7.30am to 9.30pm. Public access: Day-passes from: 10AM-4PM. Dinner: 5:30PM-9:30PM (last order at 8:30pm).

Experience

People come from all over the world to be at Bambu Indah; for their honeymoon, a special holiday, to escape a hectic world or to satisfy an addiction to adventure. Cocooned in the jungle but only minutes away from bustling Ubud, beautiful padi fields and misty mountains, Bambu Indah can satisfy even the most restless of souls

Local Produce

We grow produce on our property, often right outside your temporary vacation home. Everything prepared by our crew has either been grown close to home, or sourced from close by.

Organic Vegetable & Herb Garden

Lead by locals and gardening experts, our organic produce garden is a sustainable source of food and fosters the use of indigenous ingredients.

Homemade Delicacies

With local ingredients comes local delicacies. We channel the culture of Indonesia and sear it into all of our dishes for you to get a true taste of Bali and Bambu Indah.

Creating Karma

We believe in giving back to our community, and we strive for sustainability at every point possible. It starts with homegrown ingredients and ends on your plate. From your satisfaction to our respect for mother nature, we aim to bring it full-circle.

Feast your eyes on a real experience

The sambal is sizzling, the grill is alight, the coffee is brewing, the drinks are a delight. Come and enjoy a day by the pools. We can't wait to welcome you to our home.
