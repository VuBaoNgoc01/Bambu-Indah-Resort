Getting Here

Bambu Indah is located in the heart of the Sayan ridge, away from the bustling energy of the south, but just a 15 minutes drive from Bali’s cultural center, Ubud. We are approximately 1.5 hours North of Denpasar airport, 45 minutes from the popular Canggu, and a 2.5 hour drive from majestic waterfalls tucked away in the mountains of the North.
You’ll know you’ve arrived when you turn down a narrow village road marked by a small bamboo sign and finally reach our hidden paradise.
Check-in 2pm, check-out 12pm.
