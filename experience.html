OUT AND ABOUT

For the curious and the adventurous Bali is full of surprises and we are longing to show them to you. We can organise guided hikes and bike rides through the rice paddies or up into the mountains. And don't forget about downtown Ubud on our doorstep. It's heaving and bustling with life, with great shopping, great restaurants and fab coffee. Hire a scooter and tap into its energy and creativity. Join a pottery or batik-making course. Book a treatment with one of the amazing healers at the cosmopolitan Yoga Barn or sign up for a yoga class at Alchemy with its iconic beautiful bamboo building

Escape into the magic of old Bali with guided hikes and bike tours

Relax Renew Rejuvenate

Bambu Indah is in the vortex of one of the most healing places in the world with an energy which attracts healers from far and wide so we can offer a wide variety of treatments in our relaxing massage pods. Treatments include traditional Balinese, deep tissue and Essalen massage given by masseuses hand-picked by the Hardy family but we can also bring in experienced energy healers. Massages can also be given in the comfort of your room for a more private experience

Experience

People come from all over the world to be at Bambu Indah; for their honeymoon, a special holiday, to escape a hectic world or to satisfy an addiction to adventure. Cocooned in the jungle but only minutes away from bustling Ubud, beautiful padi fields and misty mountains, Bambu Indah can satisfy even the most restless of souls
