Stay with Us

Bambu Indah is small and intimate with six stunning, unique bamboo houses overlooking natural spring-fed pools down by the river, and four more on a luxuriant clifftop. Our ecologically mindful jungle retreat is a special place to create lifelong memories and welcomes you with an open heart

Our Houses

Decorated with artefacts collected during our travels around SE Asia and inspired by our passion for living in harmony with the environment we offer nine unique organic bamboo structures and one breathtaking Javanese bridal house. Whether you wish to sleep high up in the trees, down by the river or nestled into a jungle cliff our homes make memories to treasure.

Experience

People come from all over the world to be at Bambu Indah; for their honeymoon, a special holiday, to escape a hectic world or to satisfy an addiction to adventure. Cocooned in the jungle but only minutes away from bustling Ubud, beautiful padi fields and misty mountains, Bambu Indah can satisfy even the most restless of souls
